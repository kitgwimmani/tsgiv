About us

TSG Integrated Ventures is a wholly Canadian company that aims to spread prosperity globally through the application of sustainable technology and innovation.

Our Vision

To Promote Global Sustainability via technology, Innovation and Business.

Our Mission

To build sustainable bridges of prosperity between Canada and other nations, based on technology, innovation and entrepreneurship.

Our Core Values

Our core values are at the heart of our behavior and our approach to all engagements.

  • Collaboration: According to Helen Keller,” Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much”. Collaboration is at the core of our business model.
  • Integrity: We approach all engagements with high ethics, openness and transparency, ensuring that we always keep to the highest Canadian and Global best practices.
  • Win-Win: We strive to ensure that all engagements should be mutually-beneficial to all parties, so as to make it financially and economically sustainable in the long-term.
  • Equality: We believe in the equality of all peoples, and that all cultures can bring something to the table.
  • Creativity: We “think outside the box” and are always open to novel forms of solving problems. We are not slaves to the status-quo.
